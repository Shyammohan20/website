MOSBILL

Flagship Project

Invoice Billing & Business Management Application

📋 Overview

MOSBILL is a comprehensive business management application designed for businessmen to handle GST billing, invoicing, purchase orders, inventory management, and business analytics. The app digitizes the entire sales process with an advanced, user-friendly interface developed after extensive market research and client consultations.

Features multi-user support, real-time synchronization, comprehensive reports, and Bluetooth thermal printing capabilities using Serial Port Profile (SPP) for instant invoice printing.

🎯 Key Features

  • ✓ GST Compliant Invoicing
  • ✓ Multi-user Business Management
  • ✓ Real-time Inventory Tracking
  • ✓ Purchase Order Management
  • ✓ Business Analytics & Reports
  • ✓ Bluetooth Thermal Printing (SPP)
  • ✓ Cloud Sync with Firebase
  • ✓ Customizable for Different Business Needs
